# Influencer Campaign Performance Tracker

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Influencer Campaign Performance Tracker** is a Data Analytics and Business Intelligence project developed to analyze influencer marketing campaigns using **Python** and **Power BI**. The project helps marketing teams evaluate campaign performance, measure Return on Investment (ROI), compare influencer effectiveness, and optimize future marketing strategies through interactive dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Clean and preprocess influencer marketing data.
* Design meaningful business KPIs.
* Analyze campaign performance across different platforms.
* Segment influencers based on ROI and engagement.
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
* Provide actionable business insights and recommendations.

## 📂 Dataset

**Dataset Name:** Influencer Marketing ROI Dataset

**Source:** Kaggle

The dataset contains more than 10,000 influencer campaign records with information about platforms, influencer categories, campaign performance, reach, engagement, and sales.

---

## 📊 Project Workflow

### ✅ Phase 1 – Data Cleaning

* Imported the dataset into Jupyter Notebook.
* Removed duplicate records.
* Checked for missing values.
* Converted date columns into datetime format.
* Verified data types.
* Cleaned inconsistent values.
* Prepared the dataset for analysis.

---

### ✅ Phase 2 – KPI Design

The following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) were created:

* Total Campaigns
* Total Reach
* Total Revenue
* Total Campaign Cost
* Average ROI
* Average Engagement Rate
* Cost Per Engagement
* Conversion Rate

### ✅ Phase 3 – Analytics Investigation

* Platform vs Engagement

* Audience Group vs Performance
* KPI Cards
* Bar Charts
* Scatter Plots
* Heatmaps
* Funnel Analysis

### ✅ Phase 4 – Influencer Segmentation

## 📈 Business Insights

* Different social media platforms generate different engagement levels.
* High campaign spending does not always produce higher ROI.
* Some influencer categories generate significantly higher revenue.
* Cost-efficient influencers provide better campaign value.
* Audience segmentation improves marketing performance.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Invest more in High ROI Influencers.
* Focus campaigns on high-performing platforms.
* Increase collaboration with Cost Efficient Influencers.
* Improve campaigns with lower conversion rates.
* Continuously monitor campaign performance using Power BI dashboards.
